Client Success Story #8

Enrolled in October 2023, our client’s $12,782 debt was resolved as CLG’s dispute efforts led to the cessation of all collection attempts, providing her with needed relief.

Debt Dispute Initiation

Our client enrolled with CLG in October 2023 to resolve an outstanding debt of $12,782.00. Facing pressure from collection agencies, she turned to CLG for help in disputing the validity of the debt.

Successful Cessation

CLG promptly sent dispute letters on her behalf in November 2023, initiating the debt validation process. After ongoing correspondence, in February 2024, CLG received a letter confirming that collection efforts had ceased. This result brought our client relief and freed her from the burden of further collection attempts.
